Shotaro

Osaki Shotaro, born in Japan. He used to be a member of NCT U but quit NCT and debuted as RIIZE last year. Since his mom is a dancer, he has been learning dance since he was five years old. He had been active as a professional dancer before his debut and was the main dancer in the team. Due to his superb dancing skills his fan cam are always great to watch.







Eunseok

Song Eunseok, born in Korea. He was casted when he was on his way to school, on the subway and outside his house but refused 4 times since the casters didn’t tell him the name of the company. He is confirmed as a visual of the group.







Sungchan

Jung Sungchan, born in Korea. Similarly, he used to be a member of NCT but quit NCT and re-emerged as RIIZE last year. Similarly he was discovered by a talent scout on the road and later tried out and passed the audition to become a trainee. He is very talented in rapping and his tall height gives him a good body proportion and image.







Wonbin

Park Wonbin, born in Korea. He was a track and field athlete in high school before retiring due to injury.In 2019, he was selected by the company and had an undisclosed trainee life for four years. He serves as the center of the team and has a great voice. He also plays the guitar very well.







Sohee

Lee Sohee, born in Korea. He is the lead singer on the team. He discovered his singing talent in his freshman year of high school after following his sister, who was then enrolled in vocal school, to the People's Practical Music Academy in Ansan. He then joined SM as a trainee and debuted.
