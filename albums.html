1st Single Album-Get A Guitar

  1. Get A Guitar
  2. Memories





The pre-release single, "Memories", is a song with synthesizer and guitar sounds, and lyrics about cherishing the memories of preparing and practicing together while dreaming the same dream. "Get a Guitar", the album's title track and the group's official debut single, is a song with retro synths and funky guitar rhythm, with the lyrics that show the process of understanding and empathizing with each other and becoming a team through music, and at the same time, the members gathered in one place according to the sound of the guitar, while conveying the message that they will realize their shining dreams



Single-Talk Sexy


The single was released on October 27, 2023. Talk Saxy is a dance track with a very catchy toxic saxophone riff combined with strong 808 drums from the intro, with lyrics that express concern for the unfamiliar other in a frank and confident way of speaking that is unique to RIIZE.




Single-Love 119


Love 119 is RIIZE's single released in early 2024. It is a pop dance song that contrasts a sweet piano accompaniment with a rhythmic drum line to create a dreamy atmosphere. The lyrics of the song compare a sudden first love to an emergency situation, and the members express the emotion of first love in the style of RIIZE and the group's unique musical style, “Emotional Pop”. The dance for the chorus of the song was choreographed by members Shotaro and Won Bin.


1th Mini Album-RIIZING

  1. Boom Boom Bass
  2. Siren
  3. Impossible
  4. 9 Days
  5. Honestly
  6. One Kiss

RIIZING is RIIZE's debut mini-album released on June 17, 2024, featuring the songs “Impossible” and “Boom Boom Bass”. Impossible is a pop dance song characterized by dynamic ho-room rhythms and mysterious synthesiser atmosphere. The lyrics express that even if everyone thinks it is ‘impossible’, as long as they walk towards the same dream with each other, ‘impossible’ will become ‘impossible’ in that instant. The lyrics convey the message that even if everyone thinks it's “impossible”, as long as they are walking together towards the same dream, the “impossible” will become “possible” in that moment. Also, the choreography for the hip-hop style song Siren is very watchable and difficult. The stage for this song is very watchable.
