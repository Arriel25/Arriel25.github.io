RIIZE


RIIZE (라이즈), is a male group from SM Entertainment. Members include Shotaro, Eunseok, Sungchan, Wonbin, Sohee, Seunghan and Anton. Member Seunghan is no longer active in the group.They debuted on September 4, 2023 with their single album “Get A Guitar”. Click on the image to go to their official Instagram.



RIIZE is a combination of "Rise & Realize," which stands for a team that grows and realizes dreams together. Six members with their unique characteristics come together to create a "real-time odyssey" as a team. Based on their musical journey, RIIZE will present "Emotional Pop," an original genre expressing their own emotions and vibes to the audience.
